Our offerings

Six exceptional
peony varieties

Each variety is selected for superior bloom quality, vase life, and beauty that makes arrangements unforgettable. Available wholesale during peak Michigan season.

01

Monsieur Jules Elie

Deep rose pink

One of the most beloved peonies of all time — a full, globular double bloom in rich rose-pink with a classic, heady fragrance. Exceptionally large heads make it a showstopper in any arrangement. A perennial bestseller among florists and designers alike.

Bloom typeDouble
ColorDeep rose pink
FragranceStrong, sweet
Vase life7–10 days
02

Duchesse de Nemours

Creamy white · Ivory center

A timeless white peony with a delicate butter-yellow center that fades to pure snow white at the edges. Prized for its refined elegance, intoxicating fragrance, and clean look that pairs beautifully with any palette. A wedding and event designer's staple.

Bloom typeDouble
ColorCreamy white
FragranceModerate, pleasant
Vase life7–10 days
03

Sarah Bernhardt

Apple blossom pink

Perhaps the world's most famous peony. Large, perfectly formed double blooms in a soft, romantic apple-blossom pink — silver-tipped petals giving the flower its characteristic shimmer. Exceptional fragrance. One of the most requested varieties by name.

Bloom typeDouble
ColorSoft pink
FragranceStrong, rose-like
Vase life8–10 days
04

Shirley Temple

Soft blush to ivory

Opening a soft blush pink and maturing to a gentle ivory-white, Shirley Temple is beloved for its graceful color journey. Large, ruffled double blooms with a mild, pleasant fragrance. A sophisticated choice for garden-style and airy bridal arrangements.

Bloom typeDouble
ColorBlush to ivory
FragranceLight, delicate
Vase life7–9 days
05

Lady Alexandra Duff

Pale blush pink

An old-fashioned English rose of a peony — cup-shaped outer guard petals surrounding a tightly packed center in the softest blush. One of the most delicately beautiful peonies available. Highly sought after for luxury and high-end bridal work.

Bloom typeSemi-double / Double
ColorPale blush pink
FragranceModerate, sweet
Vase life6–9 days
06

Canary Brilliants

Soft yellow · Coral blush

Among the rarest and most coveted peony colors — Canary Brilliants opens in a soft, warm peach-yellow that glows in arrangements. A Japanese-type bloom with distinctive silky petals surrounding a dramatic center. Available in limited quantities each season.

Bloom typeJapanese / Anemone
ColorPeach yellow
FragranceLight
Vase life6–8 days
